About

Introduction

Our web forum intends on selling course material from different universities located in Canada for a cheaper price in comparison with other sources. Our webpage will contain a listing of the various universities located within Canada on the browse page. A unique aspect that our website provides is a price comparison function which lists how much the same book is selling for at the university bookstore and on amazon. Our feature gives students flexibility and choice in their academic and financial choices.

Mission Statement

Ustudy strives to provide Canadian University students with fairly priced textbooks and necessary course material to aid in the betterment of their educations. Our comparative data analysis correlates our site to other existent sources, to ensure that our users settle with the best qualitative option.
